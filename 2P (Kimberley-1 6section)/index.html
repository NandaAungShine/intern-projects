Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book travel package?

You can book our travel packages through our website, mobile app, or by contacting our customer service team directly. Online booking is available 24/7 for your convenience.

Where can I find the cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is available in the "Terms & Conditions" section of our website. You can also find it in the confirmation email sent after booking. For quick access, click here to view our cancellation policy.

Is transportation included in the package?

Most of our packages include transportation unless specified otherwise. Please check the "Inclusions" section of your selected package for details. Airport transfers are typically included for international packages.

What payment methods are accepted in Kimberly?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and in some locations, cash payments. Installment payment options are also available for selected packages.

I want to change my booking dates. How can I do this?

Please select your booking in the "My Trips" section of your account.
